Frequently asked questions

How does your AI identify shortages?

Our AI continuously monitors over 1000 global news sources, analyzing patterns and signals to identify emerging shortages before they become critical market disruptions. The system uses advanced machine learning to detect early warning signs.

What types of organizations do you work with?

We work with government agencies, large corporations, healthcare systems, and critical infrastructure organizations that need early warning of potential shortages. Our clients include Fortune 500 companies and government departments worldwide.

How quickly can you connect me with suppliers?

Our rapid brokerage system can connect you with verified suppliers within hours of a shortage alert. For enterprise clients with government connections, we can facilitate introductions within minutes during critical shortage events.

What types of shortages do you monitor?

We monitor shortages across all critical sectors including semiconductors, pharmaceuticals, medical supplies, energy resources, food commodities, and industrial materials. Our AI can be customized to focus on specific industries or product categories.

How accurate are your shortage predictions?

Our AI system has achieved 87% accuracy in predicting shortages 2-6 months in advance. We continuously improve our algorithms based on market feedback and historical data, ensuring increasingly precise predictions over time.
