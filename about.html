The evolution of our AI-powered shortage intelligence platform.

Shortage was born from a simple observation: global supply chains were constantly disrupted by unexpected shortages, but these disruptions were often predictable if you knew where to look. Our founders, experienced in both artificial intelligence and supply chain management, recognized the opportunity to revolutionize how organizations prepare for and respond to shortage events.

We built our proprietary AI infrastructure to continuously monitor thousands of global news sources, government reports, and market indicators. Our machine learning algorithms analyze patterns and signals that human analysts might miss, identifying emerging shortages weeks or months before they become critical market disruptions.

Today, Shortage serves as the critical bridge between early warning intelligence and rapid response action, connecting governments, healthcare systems, and large corporations with verified suppliers during shortage crises.

Strategic mission and vision

We envision a world where global supply chains are resilient and prepared for any disruption. Our AI-powered intelligence platform will become the standard for early warning systems, enabling organizations to anticipate and respond to shortages before they impact critical operations.

Through continuous innovation in artificial intelligence and machine learning, we aim to achieve 95% accuracy in shortage prediction, giving governments and corporations months of advance notice to secure essential supplies and maintain operational continuity.

Our vision extends beyond individual organizations to create a global network of connected intelligence, where early warning signals benefit entire industries and communities, ensuring no critical shortage goes undetected or unaddressed.

Our Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize global supply chain resilience through advanced AI-powered shortage intelligence. We continuously monitor thousands of global news sources, government reports, and market indicators to identify emerging shortages before they become critical disruptions.

We provide governments, healthcare systems, and large corporations with early warning intelligence and rapid brokerage services, connecting them with verified suppliers during shortage crises. Our goal is to ensure no critical shortage goes undetected or unaddressed.

Through cutting-edge machine learning algorithms and strategic partnerships, we deliver actionable intelligence that enables proactive decision-making, reduces supply chain risks, and maintains operational continuity during global shortage events.

Frequently asked questions

How does your AI shortage detection work?

Our AI continuously monitors thousands of global news sources, government reports, and market indicators. Advanced machine learning algorithms analyze patterns and signals to identify emerging shortages weeks or months before they become critical market disruptions.

What types of organizations do you work with?

We work with governments, healthcare systems, large corporations, and emergency response organizations. Our clients include national health agencies, Fortune 500 companies, and international relief organizations that need early warning intelligence for critical supplies.

How accurate are your shortage predictions?

Our AI algorithms achieve 95% accuracy in shortage prediction, providing organizations with months of advance notice. We continuously improve our models through machine learning and feedback from real-world shortage events.

How quickly can you connect us with suppliers during a shortage?

Our rapid brokerage service can connect you with verified suppliers within 24-48 hours of a shortage alert. We maintain a global network of pre-vetted suppliers and have direct relationships with government procurement agencies.

What makes your service different from traditional supply chain monitoring?

Unlike traditional monitoring that focuses on existing supply chains, we identify emerging shortages before they impact your operations. Our AI processes thousands of sources in real-time and provides actionable intelligence with rapid brokerage services to secure critical supplies.
